Paragraph Styles

This font is called Poppins and is free through Google Fonts.

Large paragraph style, all typography exceeds or meets WCAG 2.1 AA standards by setting (1) a maximum width on text so that it is never wider than eighty (80) characters, (2) a font-size and color combination that always ensures text is high in color contrast, and (3) adheres to the WCAG recommended line-height. Inline links are easy to see in large bodies of text and a clear interaction is shown to identify it as an actionable item.

Default paragraph style, to be used as the base typographic style, more commonly for large bodies of paragraph text. This should, however, by default apply to ALL text unless specified by unique declaration. Italic styles are provided, as well as underlines and bold. All of which inherit base styling to minimize code. Inline links are also available in most bodies of text.

Small paragraph style, to be used for content of low importance in the page hierarchy. For instance, the footer, legal copy, captions, and so on. Note that this font-size does not meet WCAG 2.1 suggested minimum font-size of 18px, or 14px and bold.
