Texas Legal Services Center - Young County Virtual Legal Clinic

Hosted by Texas Legal Services Center

Free Family Law Clinic for low-income patrons. Meet face to face with a licensed attorney over video chat.

 This is a virtual-only event.
Learn More & Register

Location

Notice: Because of the Coronavirus (COVID-19) situation, some clinic host locations are temporarily closed. However, our Family Law Clinics are still open. Register for your nearest clinic, and we will send you details on how to meet with one of our attorneys.

North Central Texas College
928 Cherry Street
Graham, TX 76450
Get Directions

Event Overview

Clinics are held every 3rd Thursday of each month from 11 a.m. - 2 p.m.

Below is a list of services that may be available:

  • Divorce
  • Custody & Visitation
  • Paternity
  • Name Changes
  • Modifications

Walk-ins welcome, but priority is given to those who pre-register.

To pre-register, please click on the following link https://www.tlsc.org/pecos

Special Instructions

Please make childcare plans accordingly. As young children are not allowed in the clinic area. If you bring a child, they must be mature enough to be unattended outside the clinic area. Thank you so much for your cooperation.
