Engineering Apollo

Getting our boys to the moon, and developing some of the greatest techniques and technologies along the way.

A visual solution that strives to restrict aesthetic resources to those available to designers from 1950-1970 (fonts, blinking cursors for interactivity, paper captions, and so on). For instance, the fonts used on this board make use of Helvetica (or fallback variants based on Helvetica) which was designed in 1953 and omnipresent in media and communications through out the following two decades.
