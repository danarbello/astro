A Case Study by West Elm

The Challenge

With a new property located in the heart of University City, Bozzuto wanted to design their model to attract young professionals and style savvy buyers. West elm stylists utilized the brand’s contemporary style and furnishings to showcase the expansive architecture and entertaining space, while also creating an atmosphere where buyers could escape and unwind from city life.

Solution

West elm's customizable living and dining room collections were used to highlight the floor plan and create welcoming entertaining spaces. The model was decorated with modern silhouettes, pops of color and sleek finishes, creating a cohesive look to appeal to all audiences. Our collection of classic mid-century and contemporary designs set the foundation for prospective buyers to feel at home.

Components used, in order of appearance: (1) Lead with Image, (2) WYSIWYG, (3) Half Image Tile, (4) Text Cards, (5) Half Image Tile, (6) Color Lead with CTA
